---
title: "Reviews"
description: "Approve / reject gates on tasks or context."
---

Tools: `request_review`, `approve`, `reject`, `list_reviews`.

Use them for handoffs between agents, a human-in-the-loop gate (human-operated agent id in Claude or Cursor), or a go/no-go before something risky.

These are room records, not GitHub PRs. Keep notes short and point at the real artifact in context.
