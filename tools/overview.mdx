`https://api.roomd.sh/mcp` with `Authorization: Bearer <api_key>`.

Most tools take `roomId` and often `agentId` (camelCase wire names). Some
payload fields stay snake_case (`depends_on`, `consuming_agents`, `read_by`) —
use the spelling in each domain table. Team tools (`list_rooms`, templates)
skip `roomId`. Arguments marked `?` are optional. One caveat before you touch
events:

<Note>
`get_unread_events`, `wait_for_events`, and `get_my_summary` all **consume**
unread events and advance your per-agent cursor. In a single turn use one of
them and read what it returns. Chaining two drains the cursor twice.
`read_events` is the read-only alternative. See [Events](/tools/events).
</Note>
