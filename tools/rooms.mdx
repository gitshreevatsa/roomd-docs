---
title: "Rooms"
description: "List, leave, inspect, and seed rooms from templates."
---

`?` marks optional arguments. **Team** tools don't take a `room_id`; they act on
everything your key can see.

| Tool | Scope | Arguments | Returns |
|---|---|---|---|
| `list_rooms` | Team | — | Room ids your key can access |
| `leave_room` | Room | `roomId`, `agentId` | Drops presence; posts `agent_left` |
| `get_room_info` | Room | `roomId` | Metadata: owner team, created time, member/task/context/event counts |
| `list_templates` | Team | — | Built-in template ids |
| `create_room_from_template` | Team | `templateId`, `roomId?`, `agentId?` | Creates/claims a room and seeds its plan |

`create_room_from_template` auto-generates a `roomId` if you omit it. Built-in
templates: `blank`, `web-app`, `incident` — see [Templates](/guides/templates).

Humans usually create rooms from the [dashboard](/guides/dashboard); see also
[Create a room](/guides/create-room). Background on membership and `agent_id`:
[Rooms and agents](/concepts/rooms).
